Every life matters

SPAN is a collaborative initiative dedicated to suicide prevention in India through research, awareness, and compassionate action. We work with individuals, families, professionals, faith communities, and institutions to promote mental well-being and to reduce the risk of suicide.
If you or someone you know is feeling overwhelmed, you are not alone. Help is available, and reaching out is a sign of strength.
If you are in immediate danger, please contact your local emergency services or a suicide helpline.
Together, we can build understanding, strengthen resilience, and create communities where life is protected and supported.

Strength is not found in suffering in silence, but in the courage to reach out for a hand. and the choice to walk together.

The primary aim of SPAN is to reduce suicide rates and suicide-related harm by bridging the gap between research evidence and real-world action, fostering a coordinated, compassionate, and informed response to suicide prevention.
