We make ways to good things happen
We believe suicide is preventable, and suicide prevention is a shared responsibility. In the Indian context, a complex interplay of social, economic, cultural, academic, occupational, and familial factors influences suicide. Recognizing this, SPAN adopts a community-oriented, culturally sensitive, and interdisciplinary approach, integrating research with grassroots action. Our work emphasizes awareness, early identification, capacity building, and system strengthening, with special attention to youth, students, women, marginalized populations, and high-risk communities.

Vision

We envision a compassionate and inclusive society where every life is valued, mental health is understood without stigma, and timely support is accessible to all, so that no one feels alone in their suffering and hope always remains possible.

Mission

The mission of SPAN (Suicide Prevention Research and Action Network) is to prevent suicide and promote mental well-being by integrating evidence-based research with community-driven action. SPAN is committed to strengthening collaboration among researchers, mental health professionals, educators, institutions, policymakers, and communities to develop, implement, and sustain culturally responsive suicide prevention strategies.

Impact

  • → Raise public awareness and reduce stigma around mental health and suicide.
  • → Strengthen gatekeeper and peer-support systems in institutions and communities.
  • → Improve early detection and timely intervention for youth and vulnerable groups.
  • → Support community-based mental health responses alongside formal healthcare.
  • → Contribute to policy, institutional practices, and public health strategies.

Prevent Suicide. Strengthen Minds. Save Lives.

SPAN integrates evidence-based research with community action to promote mental well-being, reduce suicide risk, and build compassionate, culturally responsive support systems across India.
  • Research & Evidence
  • Community Support
  • Policy & Prevention
What We Do
→ Advance research to understand suicide risk factors, protective factors, and effective prevention strategies
→ Translate research findings into practical programs, training, and interventions
→ Build strong networks for early identification, timely support, and crisis response
→ Promote awareness, reduce stigma, and encourage help-seeking behavior
→ Advocate for inclusive mental health policies and accessible care
Our Objectives
→ To promote and support relevant research on suicide, self-harm, and related mental health issues.
→ To translate research evidence into practical, community-based and institutional prevention programs
→ To strengthen early identification, referral pathways, and crisis response systems within schools, colleges, workplaces, and communities
→ To build capacity among family members, teachers, social workers, healthcare providers, community leaders, religious leaders, and peer supporters
→ To reduce stigma and improve mental health literacy and help-seeking behavior
→ To support and align with national and state mental health initiatives, including community mental health and suicide prevention policies
Our Impact
SPAN works toward creating sustainable impact primarily across India by:

→ Increasing public awareness and reducing stigma around mental health and suicide

→ Strengthening gatekeeper and peer-support systems in educational institutions and communities

→ Enhancing early detection and timely intervention, especially among youth and vulnerable groups

→ Supporting community-based mental health responses that complement formal healthcare systems

→ Contributing to policy dialogue, institutional practices, and public health strategies related to suicide prevention

By bridging research, policy, and community action, SPAN aims to contribute to a healthier, more compassionate India, where mental health is prioritized and suicide is preventable.
